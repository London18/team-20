Get Involved

Events

Join the hundreds of #TheMixHeroes taking on challenges to push the limits of their physical and mental endurance in order to support over two million young people that use our service each year. We have events for beginners as well as hardened pros.
See More

Support Us

From organising bake sales in your local area to taking quizzes in the comfort of your home, there are loads of ways you can help support The Mix, and across all our services, our cost of intervention is just £1. You can support us through Payroll Giving, setting up a monthly donation, community fundraising, and more!
See More

Work for us

Do you want to work with a team who are passionate about supporting young people and the issues that young people face? The Mix is an excellent environment to work in, so check out the jobs we have on offer and you can make a difference too.

See More

Volunteering

Join the hundreds of volunteers supporting young people. Whether you’re interested in volunteering on our helpline, helping us shape our services or developing new products and tools to help young people. We have opportunities to learn and develop, enhance your skills that are flexible, varied and make a difference to the lives of young people.
See More

Skill Up

Give your skills a boost today by completing our free online courses. Whether you want to improve your job chances or learn new ways to deal with the challenges you're facing in your life, we're here to help.
See More

Our Supporters

We’re grateful to our supporters who share our mission to empower 25s and under in the UK. If you’re interested in seeing who supports The Mix take a look at our supporter pages.
See More
